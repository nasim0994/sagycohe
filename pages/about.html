Company

Academica is a Bulgaria-based software development company specializing in eLearning solutions. With a strong foundation since its establishment, Academica focuses on enhancing the employee learning and development process for enterprises and mid-sized corporations.s

As a part of our commitment to excellence, we continuously improve our learning management system to meet and surpass the training requirements of our clients. Our core product, Academica Learning, is complemented by our expertise in custom development, allowing us to provide tailored solutions and seamless integrations to address specific training needs.

Since our inception, Academica has established itself as a prominent eLearning vendor in Bulgaria. We take pride in our track record of successfully delivering top-notch eLearning products and services to clients throughout the European Union and Middle East.

At Academica, we are passionate about making a positive impact on the way knowledge is acquired and shared within organizations worldwide. We firmly believe that effective learning experiences drive growth, productivity, and innovation. By leveraging cutting-edge technology and our expertise in eLearning solutions, we strive to revolutionize the way organizations approach knowledge transfer, enabling them to unlock their full potential. Join us on this transformative journey as we reshape the future of learning together.
