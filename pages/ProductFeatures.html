Product Features

We offer custom content design and development solutions to help you quickly get up to speed with your LMS and deliver meaningful eLearning faster

Try for free

Design

We work closely with your subject matter experts to identify your learning needs, define objectives and ensure your content meets our system functionality. We structure your content, trainee activities and assessments into a meaningful learning experience that facilitates understanding. Our system gives your raining character and elegance top notch, UX, UI, graphic, multimedia and game design.

Development

We build interactive, optimized and compatible eLearning solutions. We comply with SCORM, AICC and IMS industry standards and easily integrate with Academica Learning, as well as other learning management systems. A dedicated team of QA engineers performs end to end testing: from business logic analysis and acceptance testing, through functional and usability testing, to heuristic evaluation.

User management

User profiles let you see all data in one convenient page. There is a quick overview of the user’s learning progress as well as management details like the groups they are assigned in.

Rich content

Upload your SCORM eCourses, PDF or MS Office documents and make them a part of any training. Upload your video file or embed videos from YouTube and Vimeo. Manage the way users see them and track their usage.
